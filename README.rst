Fedora-Tagger
=============

A Flask app for helping us tag fedora packages.

Hotkeys
-------

.. hotkeys

+--------------------+----------------+
| ``j`` *or* ``↓``   | next item      |
+--------------------+----------------+
| ``k`` *or* ``↑``   | previous item  |
+--------------------+----------------+
| ``l`` *or* ``→``   | upvote tag     |
+--------------------+----------------+
| ``h`` *or* ``←``   | downvote tag   |
+--------------------+----------------+
| ``b``              | leaderboard    |
+--------------------+----------------+
| ``t``              | statistics     |
+--------------------+----------------+
| ``s``              | search packages|
+--------------------+----------------+
| ``esc``            | help           |
+--------------------+----------------+

Requires authentication.

+--------------------+----------------+
| ``i`` *or* ``a``   | add new tag    |
+--------------------+----------------+
